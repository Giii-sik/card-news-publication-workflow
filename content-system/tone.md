# Tone

Brand card-news copy should feel direct, practical, and seller-aware.

## Voice

- Clear before clever.
- Specific before broad.
- Calm urgency over alarm.
- Advice should sound like it comes from someone who understands ecommerce operations.
- Modern, useful, and lightly opinionated.
- Information sharing first; direct product CTA second.

## Good Patterns

- "먼저 확인할 것"
- "놓치기 쉬운 숫자"
- "다음 판단이 쉬워집니다"
- "매출보다 순마진을 봐야 할 때"
- "저장 안 하면 다시 찾기 어렵습니다"
- "초보 셀러가 헷갈리는 지점"

## Avoid

- Empty hype such as "혁신적인", "완벽한", "압도적인".
- Fear-only framing without a practical next step.
- Long sentences that require rereading on a phone.
