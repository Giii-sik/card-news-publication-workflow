# Research Tracks

Brand card-news production runs on five recurring tracks. The audience is fixed as beginner sellers unless a brief explicitly says otherwise.

## One-Line Standard

> Would a beginner seller lose something useful by scrolling past this card-news?

If the answer is no, narrow the topic or rewrite the angle.

## Track A: Monthly Seller Issues

- Badge: `MONTH`
- Frequency: once per month
- Use for timely operational topics such as tax season, seasonal demand, holiday promotions, and platform policy changes.
- Good hook: "이번 달 놓치면 손해보는 2가지"

## Track B: Borrow From Overseas Brands

- Badge: `BRAND`
- Frequency: twice per month
- Use one brand at a time.
- Convert brand analysis into 3 seller-copyable moves.
- Avoid general "why they succeeded" commentary.

## Track C: Revenue News In Seller Terms

- Badge: `REVENUE`
- Frequency: once or twice per month
- Break down revenue news through product mix, overseas share, D2C share, margin, and operating profit.
- The output must become a seller decision, not a business-news summary.

## Track D: AI And Tool Trends

- Badge: `TOOL`
- Frequency: twice per month
- Cover tools a seller can try immediately.
- Prioritize workflows over tool name lists.

## Track E: Seller Pain Digging

- Badge: `MARGIN`, `DETAIL`, `LONELY`, or another concrete pain badge
- Frequency: once or twice per month
- Use internal observations about loneliness, margin confusion, sourcing, consignment vs buying, and detail-page reading behavior.
- This is the Brand moat: it turns owned market observation into trust.
