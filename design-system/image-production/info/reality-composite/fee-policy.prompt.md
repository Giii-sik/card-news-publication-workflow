# Info Image - Reality Composite

## Purpose

Clean source image for an info-card image slot. The image supports the
explanation but does not carry the full card message. Typography and the lower
text box are added in HTML/CSS.

## Prompt

Create a photorealistic editorial composite image for a Korean ecommerce seller
fee-policy topic. The image should communicate: "SmartStore fees changed, and
sellers need to check what changed before they are surprised by settlement
statements."

Scene/backdrop: dark cinematic ecommerce office or small studio at night,
realistic desk environment, moody directional lighting, deep shadows, subtle
green-blue screen glow, premium news-thumbnail energy. No bottom gradient baked
in.

Subject: a fictional Korean ecommerce seller in their 30s in the foreground,
looking calm but slightly tense while holding or checking a settlement receipt
and laptop. Behind them, create an exaggerated real-photo metaphor: two
serious business/security-like people in dark suits are struggling to carry or
restrain a giant green shopping bag or parcel box with percentage symbols and
settlement papers spilling out. The metaphor should feel witty but believable,
not cartoonish.

Style: photorealistic reality composite, editorial news thumbnail, cinematic
lighting, realistic fabric and skin texture, sharp foreground, slightly softer
background, high contrast, professional but memorable.

Composition: create enough visual detail for a cropped `1080 x 819` top image
area inside a `1080 x 1350` info card. Keep the key subject in the upper and
middle area, with no required logo zone or headline zone inside the image.
Leave the lower card text box to HTML/CSS.

Avoid: no readable Korean or English text, no watermark, no real brand logo, no
Naver wordmark, no Brand logo, no typography, no page number, no fake UI, no
baked gradient overlay, no final card layout, no gore, no violence, no famous
person or public figure.
