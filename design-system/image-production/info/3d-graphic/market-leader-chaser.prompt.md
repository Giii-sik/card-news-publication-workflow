## Purpose

Info-card top image for explaining a market-race framing: Coupang as the
current leader and Naver as the close challenger.

## Visual Direction

Cinematic mascot action scene, not a small clean desk-object scene. The image
should feel closer to a high-quality GPT-generated 3D chase scene: big
expressive characters, strong motion, environment detail, practical screen
lights, smoke/dust/motion trails, and brand cues integrated into the character
body.

Image direction uses brand-feature character design, matching the hook image
system approach:

- leader: larger Coupang-like rounded mascot, white body with Coupang-style
  multicolor word/curve integrated into the front surface, rocket/delivery
  energy, running toward first place
- chaser: larger green Naver-like rounded mascot with a big molded `N`, running
  close behind with clear expression
- setting: detailed tech/logistics/control-room workspace, screens, cables,
  tools, smoke, dust, motion blur

For this design-system sample only, direct brand-feature character cues were
used after explicit user approval on 2026-05-15. Production series still need
per-series approval recorded in `_image-plan.md` before external logos or
near-logo brand marks are used.

## Reusable Prompt

Create a premium cinematic 3D character scene for a Korean Instagram info-card
image band. Use a high-quality mascot-action style: large rounded characters,
expressive eyes and brows, dynamic running poses, tactile plastic/clay material,
realistic tech/workspace environment, cinematic lighting, smoke/dust/motion
trails, foreground tools and background monitors.

Concept: Coupang is first place and Naver SmartStore is chasing.

Foreground leader: a Coupang-like mascot running ahead, white rounded square
body, confident face, small rocket/delivery pack or speed effect, multicolor
Coupang-style mark integrated as front surface printing or molded material. It
must not look like a sticker pasted on afterward.

Background chaser: a Naver-like green rounded square mascot running close
behind, anxious/determined face, large white molded `N` on the body, arms
reaching forward, visible sweat or motion cues. It must feel like a character,
not a logo panel.

Environment: detailed tech/logistics room or marketplace control room, server
racks, cables, monitors, keyboard parts, tools, warm desk surface, cool screen
light. The setting should add energy and realism, not distract from the chase.

No final card layout, no Korean headline, no large typography, no watermark, no
white sticker labels, no flat logo patches.
