# 01 Hook Background - Pixel Illustration

## Purpose

Clean source image for the fee report hook card. Typography, logo, and gradients are added in HTML/CSS.

## Prompt

Create a dark, warm, isometric pixel-art inspired illustration for a Korean ecommerce seller fee-policy topic. The image should visualize a small online store operation and settlement/fee change without any readable text.

Scene/backdrop: dark navy/charcoal digital workspace, subtle grid wall, cozy desk at night, soft warm lamps, muted shadows. No bottom gradient baked in.

Subject: a small friendly square store mascot at a desk, a green storefront/shopping bag icon with a plus symbol, a receipt/settlement paper, calculator, laptop, and small rising bar chart blocks. The mood should say “SmartStore fees changed, sellers need to check settlement details.”

Style: polished isometric pixel art / soft editorial illustration, crisp edges, charming but professional, dark background, Brand-blue accents, white/cream highlights, restrained palette.

Composition: leave the lower 35% visually darker and simpler for large Korean headline overlay; keep the main illustrated scene in the upper/middle area, centered slightly above midline. 4:5 vertical ratio.

Avoid: no readable Korean or English text, no watermark, no real brand logo, no Naver wordmark, no typography, no page number, no baked gradient overlay, no photorealism, no clutter.
