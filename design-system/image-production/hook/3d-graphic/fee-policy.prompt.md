# 01 Hook Background - 3D Graphic

## Purpose

Clean source image for the SmartStore fee-policy hook card. Typography, logo,
and gradients are added in HTML/CSS.

## Prompt

Create a premium 3D graphic hook background for a Korean ecommerce seller
fee-policy topic. The image should visualize "SmartStore fees changed, sellers
need to check settlement details" through friendly ecommerce objects rather
than readable text.

Scene/backdrop: warm modern ecommerce desk at night, laptop, monitor with
abstract charts, keyboard, receipts, calculator, parcel boxes, and soft studio
lighting. The background should feel practical, polished, and seller-focused.
No bottom gradient baked in.

Subject: a cute rounded 3D store or shopping-bag character at the center,
holding a settlement receipt and calculator. Add green storefront elements,
simple percentage-symbol objects, small bar chart blocks, and paper receipts.
The character should feel friendly but still premium and professional.

Style: polished 3D graphic, soft clay-like surfaces, realistic desk lighting,
rounded forms, crisp object details, Brand-blue accent allowed, restrained
green ecommerce accents, high-quality commercial render.

Composition: vertical 4:5 ratio. Keep the main 3D character in the upper and
middle area, centered slightly above midline. Leave the lower 35% visually
darker and simpler for large Korean headline overlay. Leave the top-left logo
area readable.

Avoid: no readable Korean or English text, no watermark, no Brand logo, no
unapproved real brand logo, no typography, no page number, no baked gradient
overlay, no final card layout, no clutter.
